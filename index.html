    What are catalogs?
    Catalogs are a great tool to organize, keep track of, and show off your things.
    Whether it’s something you like, own, want, read, heard, or saw, you can do it all creatively with a catalog.
    How do I create catalogs?
    When you find something interesting on ShopYourWay, click “Like It,” “Want It,” or “Have It” to add it to one of these three built-in lists.
    You can also click “Add to Catalog” to create any type of catalog you want.
